Boxed-In

Escape many thrilling maps with numerous puzzles and passages while dodging obstacles, arrows, and falling anvils! And don't forget the quickly rising lava - there's a lot of that!

Trailer:

This is Boxed-In! It's a Normal map and acts more like a "Practice map" for what is to come.

Watch out because lava rises in every map! Open the capsule and escape as quickly as you can!

This game is heavily inspired by Flood Escape, a game on ROBLOX made by Crazyblox.

Collaborate with up to 12 players!

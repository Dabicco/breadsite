Welcome to the Bread Gaming Server!

At Bread Gaming, we combine our love for bread, cats, and gaming to create a unique and fun experience. Our server hosts a variety of minigames and events, providing something for everyone. Whether you're exploring custom terrains in Minecraft or battling it out in the quirky game Bopl Battles, you're in for a treat!

About Us

We offer a variety of gaming experiences, including:

  • Wither Storm Anarchy Survival: A thrilling, chaotic survival mode.
  • Explorers SMP: Dive into a custom terrain world full of adventures.
  • Lemon SMP: A private world for our close-knit community.
  • Bopl Battles: Enjoy fun and frenetic slime battles in this couch/online multiplayer game.

We also host an event portal that connects to events across multiple Discord servers, including collaborations with EEGAR's server and The Decayed Team.

Join Our Community

Our vibrant community is centered around our Discord server, where we host events, share news, and connect with fellow gamers. Our dedicated moderators, including Joeyready (a cat and minigame enthusiast), Dabicco, Socoolmatteo, and Heart Kitty, ensure that everyone has a great time. We love our community, and as we say, "THE STONKS SHALL GROW!"

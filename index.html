Welcome to the Bread Gaming Server!

At Bread Gaming, we blend our love for bread, cats, and gaming into a unique experience. Our server offers a variety of minigames and events, including:

  • Wither Storm Anarchy Survival: A chaotic survival mode with epic challenges.
  • Explorers SMP: A custom terrain world full of adventures.
  • Lemon SMP: A private world for our close-knit community.

Our event portal, a lobby within the Minecraft server, connects you to these Minecraft servers. Additionally, we host:

  • Bopl Battles: Exciting slime battles in a fun couch/online multiplayer game.

The event portal also provides access to events across various Discord servers, such as EEGAR's server and The Decayed Team.

About Us

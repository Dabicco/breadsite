Welcome to the Bread Gaming Server!

At Bread Gaming, we mix our love for bread, cats, and gaming into a unique and engaging experience. Our server offers a range of minigames and events, specifically crafted to bring joy to our community members. We focus on both casual and competitive play, providing something for everyone.

Minecraft Events

Our Minecraft server is the heart of our gaming activities, offering various exciting modes:

  • Wither Storm Anarchy Survival: A chaotic survival mode where players face the formidable Wither Storm, battling for survival in a lawless world.
  • Explorers SMP: An adventure-driven server featuring a custom terrain world, perfect for explorers and builders alike. Dive into a rich landscape filled with unique biomes and challenges.
  • Lemon SMP: A private, close-knit community world where players can collaborate and build without the threat of external chaos.

Access all these amazing Minecraft experiences through our event portal, a lobby that serves as the gateway to each of these unique worlds. The portal also connects to special events across various Minecraft servers, including collaborations with EEGAR's server and The Decayed Team.

Bopl Battles

Bopl Battles is a delightful addition to our game offerings. This fun couch/online multiplayer game lets players control adorable slimes in fast-paced battles. We regularly host tournaments and special events, encouraging both new and experienced players to join the fun and showcase their skills.
